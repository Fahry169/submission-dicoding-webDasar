FC Barcelona, commonly known as Bar√ßa, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Founded in 1899 by a group of footballers led by Joan Gamper, the club has since grown into one of the most successful and widely supported teams globally. The club's signature blue and garnet colors, earning them the nickname "Blaugrana," are proudly represented on their crest, which features elements like the St. George Cross, the Catalan flag, and a football. Over the years, Barcelona has claimed numerous trophies, including multiple La Liga titles, Copa del Rey victories, and UEFA Champions League triumphs, cementing their legacy as one of the premier clubs in football history.

The current FC Barcelona jersey for the 2023-2024 season, designed by Nike, reflects a blend of tradition and modernity. The home kit features the club's iconic Blaugrana colors with bold vertical blue and garnet stripes, reminiscent of the classic designs from the early 2000s. This timeless look is combined with subtle contemporary elements, making it both a nod to the past and a step forward in style. The front of the jersey prominently displays Spotify as the main sponsor, a partnership that began in 2022, with the logo appearing in white text across the chest.
